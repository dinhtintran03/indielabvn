Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-02

This Privacy Policy describes how Indie Lab VN ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile applications and this website.

Scope

This policy applies to all Indie Lab VN apps and this site. Examples include, but are not limited to: SimpliCoin and other minimal utility apps we publish.

What information we collect

We design our apps to collect no personal data from you. Specifically:

  • No account creation and no profile data.
  • No analytics SDKs and no advertising SDKs.
  • No server-side storage of your content.

Some apps may process data locally on your device to provide features (for example, calculations, preferences, offline data). Such data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

App permissions

Our apps request only the permissions required for core functionality. Examples:

  • Internet: used solely to access necessary online resources (for example, store listing or update checks) when applicable.
  • Storage/Photos/Media: only when a feature allows importing or exporting files you choose.
  • Notifications: only if the app provides optional alerts you opt into.

Permission access is used strictly for the stated purpose and not for tracking.

Third‑party services

By default, our apps do not integrate third‑party analytics or advertising services. If a specific app uses a third‑party service for essential functionality, the service and purpose will be clearly disclosed inside that app and, if required, within this page.

Children’s privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete the information.

Data retention

Data processed locally by the app remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not keep copies on our servers.

Your choices

  • You can control or revoke app permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You can delete locally stored app data via in‑app options (if provided) or by uninstalling the app.

Security

We use industry‑standard practices to protect this website. For our apps, we minimize data collection to reduce risk. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the app or on this site.
